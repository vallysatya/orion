# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Orion a welcoming, harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity,
sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience level,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Preferring precise technical honesty over hype

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others’ private information without permission
- Sharing secrets, credentials, or private data in issues/PRs
- Sexualized language or imagery
- Other conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior via GitHub by contacting the maintainers through
a private channel (Security advisory for security matters, or maintainer contact
on the repository for conduct issues). Maintainers will review and respond as
appropriate.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits,
issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and when an individual is
officially representing the project in public spaces.
